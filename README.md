DEBASEMENT (DBS) — an AGORA teardown, render-not-invent, ENHEDUANNA BASELINE. ⚑ A monetary standard erodes two ways that must never be collapsed: of the STANDARD (less metal for the same name — light coins, short-weight, a clipped rim) and by ALLOY (same weight, silver swapped for copper). Alloy debasement needs a struck coin passing by its face, so it is the YOUNGER form; standard-erosion is as old as the weighed shekel — which is why Enheduanna's coinless world (no coin for ~1,700 yrs) could only be cheated the first way. ⚑ THREE live machines: a two-series Roman denarius fineness BAND (surface/Walker vs bulk-core/Butcher-Ponting — the ~15-point method gap drawn as width, not hidden; near-pure under Augustus to a few-percent billon by the 260s); a DEBASE-THE-MINT toy showing Gresham's Law is CONDITIONAL — good money flees only under a mispriced legal-tender peg and reverses (Thiers) when people may discount the bad coin; and a two-attacks coin (clip the rim vs swap in copper). Two-layer: SETTLED (two forms + ordering, Nero AD64 first debasement, antoninianus fiduciary from day one, method-variance, Gresham's preconditions) vs CONTESTED (exact fineness is method-dependent; debasement to collapse is not a proven sole cause; Gresham is not universal; ancient weights were BOUNDED not runaway ~5-6pct variation; inflation at 2300 BCE is anachronistic). ⚠ Enheduanna is ERA + SCRIPT only, never a mint official; seigniorage names who gains (the antoninianus hid a 25pct cut).
